How it Works

Riddler is a three step game that involves chance, cordination-dexterity and quick thinking in the end.
It's therefore so named seeing as it ends with a test of wit, where based on the points accumulated, a set of riddles is posed.

Instructions:

Level 1: The first level is all about chance, like a slot machine in a casino where you insert a coin and roll to play.
Here, you enter a number, any number between 1 and 9 is your 'currency' and then submit to roll/play.
The site generates three random numbers and displays the in a sequence representing one of the following outcomes:

  • All three numbers match - This represents the highest possible score of a hundred points
  • Any two of the numbers match - This represents half of the possible score
  • Different numerical outcome in all three instances - This only only gets you the bare minimum points of participation, ten points.
Good Luck!
