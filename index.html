How it Works

Riddler is a three step game that involves chance, co-ordination-dexterity and a combination of wits and quick thinking in the end.

It's thus named seeing as it ends with the posing of riddles, where based on the points accumulated, a set of riddles is posed.

Instructions:

Level 1

The first level is all about chance, like a slot machine in a casino where you insert a coin and roll to play.
Here, you enter a number, any number between 1 and 9 is your 'currency' and then submit to roll/play.
The site generates three random numbers and displays the in a sequence representing one of the following outcomes:

  • All three numbers match - This represents the highest possible score of a hundred points
  • Any two of the numbers match - This represents half of the possible score
  • Different numerical outcome in all three instances - This only only gets you the bare minimum points of participation, ten points.
Once done, proceed to the next level which will have very little to do with luck, if any at all. Good Luck!
